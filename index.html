About Us

Hotel Goura, Daringbadi offers a peaceful retreat in the lap of nature, often called the "Kashmir of Odisha". Ideally located for travelers exploring the scenic beauty of Daringbadi, the hotel features well-maintained rooms equipped with essential amenities for a comfortable stay. Guests can enjoy local Odia delicacies and popular dishes at our in-house restaurant. The property also provides cozy common areas, attentive service, and a serene ambiance to help you relax. Whether you are visiting with family or friends, Hotel Goura promises a memorable experience with warm hospitality.
