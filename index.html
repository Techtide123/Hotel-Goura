About Us

Coco Heritage Baga Resort is an exquisite hotel that offers a perfect blend of luxury and comfort. Located in the heart of Baga, it is an ideal destination for travelers looking to explore the beautiful beaches and buzzing nightlife of Goa. The resort boasts elegantly furnished rooms, well-equipped with modern amenities to cater to the needs of guests. The in-house restaurant serves delicious local and international cuisines prepared by expert chefs. The hotel also features a swimming pool, spa, and fitness center for guests to relax and rejuvenate. With its stunning architecture and warm hospitality, Coco Heritage Baga Resort promises an unforgettable stay for all.
